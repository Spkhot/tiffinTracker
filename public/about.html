What is TiffinTracker?

TiffinTracker was born from a simple problem: manually counting daily meals is a hassle. It's easy to forget, leading to confusion and disputes with your tiffin service provider at the end of the month. Our mission is to automate this process completely.

We provide a hassle-free system that sends you a quick notification at your meal times. With a single tap, your meal for that day is logged. Your monthly count and bill are updated in real-time on your personal dashboard, giving you complete clarity and peace of mind.

How It Works

1

Sign Up & Verify

Create your account in seconds and verify your email. This ensures your account is secure and you receive all notifications.

2

Complete Your Setup

Tell us your tiffin service's name, the price per meal, and what times you receive your tiffins. This personalizes the tracker for you.

3

Respond & Relax

That's it! You'll get a simple "Yes/No" notification at your set meal times. Just respond, and we'll handle all the counting for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is TiffinTracker free to use?

Yes! TiffinTracker is completely free for personal use. Our goal is to provide a simple solution to a common problem.

What if I forget to respond to a notification?

No problem! Any missed notifications will appear in the "Pending Actions" section on your dashboard. You can go there anytime to mark whether you took the tiffin or not.

Can I change my tiffin times or price later?

Absolutely. You can edit your mess name, price, and meal times at any point from your profile menu on the dashboard.
